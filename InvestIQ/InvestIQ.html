Finance 101 — Know the Basics
📈
What is a Stock?
A stock represents ownership in a company. When you buy a share, you become a part-owner. If the company grows, your share value goes up.
💹
What is a Mutual Fund?
Money pooled from many investors managed by a professional. It invests in stocks, bonds etc. based on the fund's objective.
📊
What is SIP?
Systematic Investment Plan — investing a fixed amount every month. Benefits from rupee cost averaging and the power of compounding.
₿
What is Crypto?
Digital currency secured by cryptography. Bitcoin and Ethereum are the most popular. High risk, high reward — only invest what you can afford to lose.
⚖️
Risk vs Reward
Higher returns always come with higher risk. Balance your portfolio between high-risk equity, moderate mutual funds, and low-risk debt.
🔄
Compound Interest
Earning returns on your returns. ₹10,000 at 12% for 20 years grows to ₹96,463. Start early, stay invested.
